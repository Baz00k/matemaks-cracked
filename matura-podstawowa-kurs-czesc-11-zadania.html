B.\( x=2 \)
C.\( x=3 \)

A

C.\( \frac{4}{7} \)

B.\(\frac{11}{2} \)
C.\(\frac{2}{11} \)
D.\(11 \)

C.\( m=4 \)
D.\( m=5 \)

C.\( x=2 \)
D.\( x=\frac{25}{11} \)

B.\( -4 \)
C.\( -3 \)
D.\( -2 \)
